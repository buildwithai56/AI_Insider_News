# Content Sourcing & Publishing Plan

## Source Channels
### Primary Sources
- **Research**: arXiv, MIT Technology Review, Nature Machine Intelligence.
- **Tech News**: TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired.
- **Official Blogs**: OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Anthropic, NVIDIA.

### Secondary Sources
- **Social Media**: Reddit (r/MachineLearning, r/ArtificialIntelligence), Twitter (X).
- **Video**: YouTube (Computerphile, AI Explained).

## Content Types
1. **Daily Briefing**: 5-10 key updates from the last 24 hours.
2. **Deep Dives**: Explaining complex research papers (e.g., GPT-5 architecture).
3. **Tool Spotlights**: Reviewing new AI products and productivity tools.
4. **Policy & Ethics**: Coverage of AI regulation (e.g., EU AI Act).

## Publishing Schedule
- **Real-time News**: Published as discovered.
- **Tool of the Week**: Every Monday.
- **Weekly Roundup Podcast**: Every Friday.
- **Monthly Research Review**: Last Sunday of each month.

## Quality Control
- **AI Formatting**: Automatic headers, bullet points, and summaries.
- **Plagiarism Check**: Content recreation engine ensures 100% original text.
- **Human Oversight**: Mandatory admin review for "high impact" stories.
